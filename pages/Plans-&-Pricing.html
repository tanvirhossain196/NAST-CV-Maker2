Frequently Asked Questions

What is NastCV?

NastCV is a free online resume builder designed to help you create professional-looking resumes quickly and easily. Our platform guides you through the process of building a compelling resume that stands out to potential employers.

Is NastCV really free?

Yes, NastCV is 100% free. Your first resume, including all design features and unlimited downloads, comes at no cost. We believe in making professional resume creation accessible to everyone.

How can NastCV be free?

We offer a freemium model where basic features are completely free. We generate revenue through optional premium features and partnerships, allowing us to provide a high-quality service without charging users.

Can I create different versions of my resume?

Absolutely! NastCV allows you to create and save multiple resume versions tailored to different job applications. You can easily switch between templates and customize each version to suit different roles or industries.

How does NastCV ensure my data is safe?

We take data privacy seriously. All your information is encrypted and stored securely. We comply with GDPR regulations and never share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Does NastCV support multiple languages?

Currently, NastCV supports English, Spanish, and French. We're continuously working on expanding our language options to make resume creation accessible to more users globally.

Can I also create cover letters?

Yes! In addition to resume templates, NastCV offers professional cover letter templates that complement your resume. You can create and customize cover letters to match your resume design.
